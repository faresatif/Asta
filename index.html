Asta

Asta is an orphan who grew up in a small village called Hagi. He is known for his brave personality and dream of becoming the Wizard King. Although he does not possess any magical abilities, he relies on his physical strength and ability to use anti-magic swords

Yuno

Yuno is another orphan who grew up with Asta, and he is the complete opposite. He has an amazing magical talent and an exceptional ability to use wind, making him one of the leading candidates to become the Wizard King.

Black Clover

The anime takes place in a fantasy world where people rely on magic in their daily lives. Magicians compete for higher status and to achieve their dreams, with dangers threatening the peace of the world.
